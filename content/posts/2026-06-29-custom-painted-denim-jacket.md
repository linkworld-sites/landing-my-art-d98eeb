## The Commission Process: Four Steps from Brief to Your Door

Commissioning a hand-painted jean jacket at My Art follows a structured four-step process. It's designed to give you full visibility and creative input — and to protect the quality of the final piece.

### Step 1 — The Brief

You share everything: what you want, references you love, colours that matter to you, and the meaning behind the piece. This can be a portrait, an animal, a landscape, a lyric, a symbol, or something harder to name. There are no wrong briefs — only honest ones.

### Step 2 — Sketch Approval

The artist sketches a layout and proposes a palette. You review it. You suggest changes. Nothing touches the denim until you've signed off. This step is included in every pricing tier.

### Step 3 — Layer-by-Layer Painting

Work begins on your jacket. You receive progress photos throughout the painting process — not just a final reveal. You see the piece come alive, layer by layer.

### Step 4 — Heat-Sealed Delivery

Once painting is complete, the acrylics are heat-sealed for permanence. Your jacket ships with a Certificate of Authenticity and care instructions.

## Bring Your Own Jacket or Source Vintage

You have two options for the denim itself:

**Bring your own jacket** — most clients supply a jacket they already own and love. It could be a vintage find, a gift, or something you've worn for years that's ready for its second life.

**Vintage sourcing** — if you don't have a jacket, My Art can source curated vintage denim for an additional $40–$80. The canvas matters. Vintage denim takes paint differently than off-the-shelf — the texture, weight, and pre-existing character become part of the final piece.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is a custom painted denim jacket machine washable?**
Yes. Every My Art jacket is painted with professional textile acrylics and then heat-sealed after completion. The paint is permanently bonded to the fabric — it won't crack or fade in a normal machine wash cycle.

**How long does a hand-painted jean jacket commission take?**
Canvas tier (single motif) takes 7–10 days. Signature tier (full jacket) takes 10–14 days. Bespoke commissions run on a priority production timeline discussed at briefing.

**Can I commission a portrait on a denim jacket?**
Yes — portrait work is one of My Art's specialisms. Gallery-level portrait commissions fall under the Bespoke tier (from $480). The artist has the rare fine-art skill combined with textile medium expertise required to execute portraits at this level.

**Do I need to supply the jacket?**
No. You can bring your own denim or add vintage sourcing ($40–$80) to your commission. Both paths produce exceptional results.

**Is the jacket registered as an original artwork?**
Every Bespoke commission ships with a Certificate of Authenticity registering the piece as an original artwork. Canvas and Signature pieces are unique by nature — no two are ever identical.

**Can I commission a bridal denim jacket?**
Yes. Bridal commissions — painted vows, dates, portraits — are among the most emotionally significant pieces the studio produces. These fall under the Bespoke tier and are given the full fine-detail treatment.
