The Process

From Vision to Wearable Art

We collaborate closely with you to turn your idea into a museum-worthy piece you'll actually wear.

01
Share Your Vision

Tell us your idea, references, colours, and what this jacket means to you. No brief is too wild.

02

Our artist sketches a layout and palette. You review, refine, and approve before a single brush touches denim.

03
The Painting

Layer by layer, your jacket comes to life using fabric-safe acrylic paints that flex, breathe, and last for years.

04
Delivered to You

Heat-sealed and packaged with care. Your finished piece arrives ready to wear and ready to turn heads.
